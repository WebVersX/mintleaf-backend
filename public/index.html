Insider Tips

Know Before You Go

A few friendly tips from our regulars to make your visit perfect.

Parking is tight

We have limited parking available. We recommend arriving a bit early, especially on weekends, or using a ride-sharing service.

Chicken Dishes May Vary

To ensure freshness, our chefs sometimes create unique variations of chicken dishes. It's always delicious, but may differ slightly from the menu!

Biryani is Recommended!

Our guests consistently rave about our Biryani. If you're visiting for the first time, it's a can't-miss dish.
